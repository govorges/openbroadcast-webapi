What is OpenBroadcast?

OpenBroadcast is a Roku channel that delivers user-uploaded content.

How do I use it?

You can upload videos to OpenBroadcast hereopen_in_new and you can view uploaded videos on the OpenBroadcast Roku Channelopen_in_new.

Who made it?

OpenBroadcast was made by one person (hi), with some consulting help from the people at SCREWopen_in_new.

If you want to contact the creator of OpenBroadcast, you can do so through the email address in the footer.

How was it made?

OpenBroadcast is made as a proof of concept personal project for learning purposes, so I tried to use some stuff I was unfamiliar with.

Currently, my stack includes: SCSS, Flask/Gunicorn, Docker, OpenCV2, & Postgres.

What keeps it running?

OpenBroadcast is supported primarily by ads on the channel that run every once in a while between videos.

Why was it made?

It was made to kind of hit that "Old Internet" niche, where people would share content just because they thought it was interesting, not for virality.
