Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is OpenBroadcast? OpenBroadcast is a video sharing platform delivered through Roku channels. It allows users to upload their own videos and watch videos uploaded by others.
Q: How does it work? You can add the OpenBroadcast channel to your Roku device and view a feed of videos uploaded by other users. If you want to, you can also upload your own videos.
Q: What can I upload? If you are allowed to share your content and it follows Roku's Terms of Service and abides by US laws & regulations, anything goes.
Q: Who runs OpenBroadcast? OpenBroadcast is built and operated by one person (hi). As you can see, I am not a web developer.
Q: How can I report an issue? You can find information about reporting issues here open_in_new.
