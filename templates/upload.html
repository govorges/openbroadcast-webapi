How it Works

Simply select a video (.mp4) file from your machine and then edit the title and description to your liking.

We have a few rules:

  • Keep it PG-13! Roku has strict guidelines on NSFW (Not-Safe-For-Work) content.
  • No affiliate advertisements! We're trying to keep things wholesome around here, and if everyone's going around advertising an affiliate program it'll get really annoying really quickly.
  • (You can, however, advertise your social media handles. Go crazy!)
  • Nothing that breaks the Roku Terms of Service is allowed on our channel.
  • Only post content you own the rights to, and by posting your content to OpenBroadcast you give us your express permission to use it unless otherwise revoked (see /reports/).
